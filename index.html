Marcus Aurelius

A roman Emperor known as one of the Five Good Emperors.
Marcus was also a well known Stoicist, following a
branch of Philosophy called Stoicism that maximizes
positive emotions, reduces negative emotions and helps
individuals to hone their virtues of character.

Meditations

Meditations
was a book written by the Emperor himself
his diary, depicting his day to day life
as he was on campaign in central Europe.
